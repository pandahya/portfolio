TOY POLYHEDRON

2019

Designing an constructing a polyhedron-based interactive object with a "surprise" inside. The outside is neutral toned wood, the inside is colorful man-made and industrial materials.
Assignment concept based on Froebel Gifts. No glue allowed; joining methods must be mechanical.

Materials

  • wood (basswood & cherry) sheets
  • rivets
  • aluminum sheet metal
  • felt (green, blue, white)
  • stuffing
  • PVC sheets (red, yellow)
  • acrylic mirror sheet
  • wire mesh
  • hinges + screws
  • round magnets
  • wooden dowels + nails
  • washers
