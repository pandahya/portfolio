EGG-LOVING

2021

An investigation on the egg-loving subculture, in the form of a zine.
*All images and body text used are not mine.

"Egg-loving culture" is an extremely informal and mostly unestablished community of people who like eggs as an object or novelty item more than something to eat. My zine explores the way these people may do so, in art, clothing, daily life, social feed, etc. The information is presented mostly via images, but some text is provided to explain a bit about the appeal of eggs and add short commentary.

Design Process

In envisioning my final product, I really wanted something with a tactle feel but through a digital medium.
