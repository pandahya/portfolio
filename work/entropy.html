Every year, the Design & Technology department at Parsons organizes a series of events to celebrate and showcase the graduating students and thesis projects of that year (BFA + MFA). The events are organized by committees of students in the graduating class and a faculty advisor, and include a pop up show, screening, symposium, reader/publication, and website.

Entropy was chosen as the theme of this yearâ€™s showcase to embody the chaos and resilience our class of graduates experienced during our time in the program. Entropy, a physics concept, refers to a measure or state of disorder, randomness, or uncertainty. But for us, it represents the beauty of chaos and the power of creativity.
