OLYMPICS INFOGRAPHICS

2022

Documenting and visualizing Champions at the Beijing 2022 Winter Olympics with Instagram-style square panels.

Covers Ice Hockey: Women's Gold Medal Game; Figure Skaing: Mixed Pairs; Snowboard: Men's Big Air; Biathalon: Men's 15km Mass Start

Process

The goal was to first create an almost complete set of graphics beforehand that would act like a "template" so I could easily input the data quickly after the actual data is released.
I started with three moodboards to work out a visual style I liked. I ended up combining the first and last for a scientific feel with tactile/scrapbook-y visuals not traditionally seen in data viz. I wanted to make my infographics feel fun and approachable but also informative and accurate.

I used the previous Winter Olympic's data to first make my layouts. To account for unexpected results like a tie or overtime, I also drafted additional layouts.


I assigned a color and pattern to each sport to accentuate the mood of each, and tried to encorporate them into the design of the graphics as well.
